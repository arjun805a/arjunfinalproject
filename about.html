What Is the Memory Match Game?

This is a simple and fun browser-based memory game. Your task is to flip two cards at a time and try to match pairs. The goal is to find all matching pairs with the fewest moves possible.

How to Play

  • Click on any card to flip it over.
  • Try to find a matching emoji on another card.
  • If the cards match, they stay flipped.
  • If not, they flip back over so remember them!
  • Match all the pairs to win the game.
  • The fewer moves you make, the better your memory!

Fun Fact

Can you guess how many combinations are possible with 8 pairs?

There are 16! / (2!8) = 10,395,360 combinations!
